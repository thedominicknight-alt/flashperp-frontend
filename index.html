// How It Works

Four steps.
Under five minutes.

FlashPerp combines uncollateralised flash lending with time-bounded perpetual trading into a single tight loop.

01
BORROW
Borrow up to 80% of pool liquidity instantly. No collateral required. A 0.3% borrow fee is charged at open.
02
TRADE
Open a long or short with up to 10x leverage. Entry price locked via Pyth Network sub-second oracle.
03
WAIT
Your position runs for 1 minute to 1 hour. The risk engine monitors your health factor in real time.
04
SETTLE
At expiry the position auto-settles. Profit goes to you. Principal returns to the pool. Zero manual steps.
