How It Works
01
Deposit USDC
Deposit any amount of USDC into the pool and receive LP shares proportional to your contribution.
02
Back Flash Trades
Traders borrow from your pool to open flash positions. They pay 0.3% borrow fee per trade.
03
Earn Fees
Borrow fees accumulate in the pool, increasing your share value automatically. No claiming needed.
04
Withdraw Anytime
Burn your LP shares to withdraw USDC + accumulated yield. No lock-up period on testnet.
⚠ Testnet only. LP deposits are protected by the insurance fund and utilisation ceiling (max 80% borrowed). In extreme market conditions, bad debt may reduce LP returns.
